League of Legends!

This is my page discussing the importance of minion control on League of Legends.

You must be able to effectively control when you are pushing and when you are freezing to increase your chances of winning!

Some general guidelines to adhere to would be if the enemy is not in lane doing other objectives, PUSH, if the enemy is not in lane but is on their way, FREEZE.
